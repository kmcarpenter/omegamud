Configuration
Config is used to toggle a few commands. These commands include:
AUTOEXIT - allows your character to automatically see all the exits of the room.
AUTOGOLD - your character will automatically take the gold from a creature you have just killed.
AUTOLOOT - your character will automatically take all the equipment that the creature has on it at the time of it's death.
AUTOSAC - the creature you have just killed will automatically be sacrificed to the golds for a miniscule reward.
ANSI - will allow you to see the game in color or not depending on if the application/software you use to play can handle color.
KEEP - allows to to keep your custom title (the trailer text when checking the wholist). More help on titles will be available in the MISC section of help.
BLANK - will either insert a blank line before your prompt or not.
BRIEF - allows you to see room description upon entering the room.
COMBINE - allows you to see object lists in combined format.
PROMPT - allows you to have a prompt (see Prompt Help about prompt).
TELNETGA - allows you to recieve a telnet GA sequence
COMBAT - with this command toggled on, you can see everything that happens in combat. With it off, you will not see any action that does not result in damage.
To view the status of these commands, during the game, type config. To change there status to off, type config -<option_to_toggle> and to toggle it on, use a + rather than the -.

The options of:
AUTOEXIT, AUTOLOOT, AUTOSAC, BLANK, BRIEF, or COMBINE
can also be toggle just by typing the command.
