Grouping
Grouping is just as it sounds. Two or more people join together to form a group. As a group, the group members will work as a team to kill creatures/player and will split the experience.
Commands
Use of Command
follow <player> This will allow you to follow a character as in, where ever they go, you shall follow automatically. In order to stop following them, you will need to type follow self.

A character of a higher level can follow any character lower than themselves where as a character that is lower level can only follow a character up to 5 levels higher than their own.
group and group <player> group used alone will allow you to check the status of everyone currently in your group.

group <player> when used, will group a player that is currently following you. If you use the command on someone that is currently in your group, this will remove them from your group as well as force them to stop following you.
gtell <message> This command is used to send a message to the people in your group.
split <amount> This splits some gold between you and all the members of your group who are in the same room as you. This isn't necessary but you should split it.
