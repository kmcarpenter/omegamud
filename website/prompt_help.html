Prompt
Commands
Use
prompt Without an argument afterwards, this command will either show or hide your prompt.
prompt all This command will give you the standard prompt that looks like <HitPoints Mana MovementPoints>.
prompt <%X> By replacing the X with one of the characters listed below, it will decide which value will be displayed:
h current hitpoints
H maximum hitpoints
m current mana
M maximum mana
v current movement points
V maximum movement points
x your current experience points
g current amount of gold you are holding
a your current alignment
r the name of the room you are currently in
R vnum of the room you are currently in (IMMORTALS ONLY)
z area name that you are currently in (IMMORTALS ONLY)
So, you want to see an example of making a prompt?
prompt <%h hp %m m %v mv>

will look like this in the game: <10 hp 100 mana 100 mv>
