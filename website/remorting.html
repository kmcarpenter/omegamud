Remorting
Remorting is also know as multiclassing.

Upon reaching level 100, your character may opt to REMORTALIZE into a new class. To do this, simply find the Class Master and use the REMORTALIZE command to become a multiclassed character. When you successfully use this command, you will return to level 2 and will keep your old equipment. You will not be use any of it unless it is equal to or below your (current) level. When you log off, you will have this equipment when you return. When advancing in the new class, you will learn new skils and spells that are in the skill list of your new class. The skills and spells that you learned as your first class will be automatically learnt when you approach that level. If your new class (or old class) have the same skill or spell in it at different levels, if you practiced it in your first class, it will be learnt automatically at the first occurance of the skill in either of the skill lists.

If your first class used blood points rather than mana, when you REMORTALIZE, if your new class would use mana, you will continue to use blood points. The same applies for vica versa.
A multiclassed character will only gain half the experience of a single classed character as well as a single class character will also recieve an extra practice over a multiclassed character per level.
For example, if a single class character was to recieve maximum experience points for a kill (450), for the multiclassed character, it would only recieve 225.

Syntax for remortalizing
remortalize -=class=- pkiller
remortalize -=class=- peaceful

To REMORTALIZE to a new class yet maintain the same PKiller status (if peaceful remorting peaceful or if PK remorting PK), it will cost 2,000,000 gold and 500 quest points.
To REMORTALIZE to the opposite PKiller status (peaceful to PK or PK to peaceful), it will cost 5,000,000 gold and 750 quest points.

***
YOU MUST BE IN THE PRESENCE OF A CLASS MASTER FOR THE REMORTALIZE COMMAND TO WORK
